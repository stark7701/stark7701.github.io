Plays everything

MP4, MKV, AVI, MOV, FLV, WEBM and more — with advanced audio (AC3, DTS, TrueHD) via built-in decoders. 4K & HDR ready.

Swipe for brightness, volume and seeking. Double-tap to skip, pinch to zoom, long-press for quick speed.
